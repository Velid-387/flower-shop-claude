O nama

Bloom & Petal was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to share the beauty of nature through artfully crafted floral arrangements.

Our team of passionate florists brings years of experience and creativity to every bouquet we create. We source our flowers from local and sustainable farms whenever possible, ensuring that each bloom is fresh and environmentally conscious.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, expressing your feelings, or simply bringing some natural beauty into your home, we're here to help you find the perfect floral expression.

Najčešće postavljena pitanja

How can I place an order?
You can place an order by visiting our website and clicking on the "Order" button. Once you're logged in, you can select the desired products and proceed to checkout.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. You can choose the preferred method during checkout.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 14-day return policy. If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer support team for assistance.
Can I track my order?
Yes, you can track your order by logging into your account and visiting your order history.
